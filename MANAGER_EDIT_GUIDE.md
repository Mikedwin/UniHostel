## How to Edit Your Hostel Listing

### Step 1: Access the Edit Page
1. Login to your Manager account
2. Go to your Manager Dashboard
3. Find your hostel in the "My Listings" section
4. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) on your hostel card

### Step 2: Update Basic Information
You can edit:
- **Hostel Name** - Change the name of your hostel
- **Location** - Update the address or area
- **Description** - Modify the hostel description
- **Hostel Image** - Upload a new main image (optional, max 500KB)

### Step 3: Update Room Details
For each room type, you can:
- **Change Price** - Update the semester price (in GH‚Çµ)
- **Adjust Capacity** - Set how many students can be accommodated
- **Select Facilities** - Click on facility buttons to add/remove amenities

### Step 4: Manage Facilities
Click on facility buttons to toggle them on/off:
- **Blue button** = Selected
- **White button** = Not selected

Available facilities include:
- Basic Amenities: WiFi, AC, Hot Water, Furnished
- Security: Security, CCTV, Secure Entry
- Shared Spaces: Kitchen, Study Room, Common Area, Gym
- Services: Laundry, Cleaning Service, Parking

### Step 5: Save Changes
1. Review all your changes
2. Click the **"Save Changes"** button at the bottom
3. You'll be redirected back to your dashboard
4. Your changes are now live!

## üí° Tips

### Image Upload
- Keep images under 500KB for best performance
- Use clear, well-lit photos of your hostel
- Landscape orientation works best

### Pricing
- When you change room prices, pending applications are automatically updated
- Already paid applications keep their original price

### Capacity
- Current occupancy is shown (e.g., "5/20 Occupied")
- You can increase capacity but not below current occupancy
- System prevents overbooking automatically

### Facilities
- Select all facilities that apply to each room type
- More facilities = more attractive to students
- Be honest about what's available
